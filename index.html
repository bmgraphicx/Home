PROFILE

BACKGROUND

A skilled Graphic Designer with over nine years of experience with extensive knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite. I have worked with mixed media, software and have used coding to create designs from concept to completion. I have experience working on a wide variety of projects including magazine designs both in print and digital including video, logo rebrands, book covers for a published author, typeface designs from scratch and have worked online with HTML emails, web graphics and banners. I have worked on advertising and liaised directly with clients. I have built my own website and am constantly updating my skills and taking on new challenges. I am used to working to strict deadlines as well as assisting others in the workplace. I am self-motivated, hardworking and reliable and always willing to take on a new challenge and continue to update and expand my skill set.
